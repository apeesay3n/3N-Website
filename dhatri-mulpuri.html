About Dhatri

Dhatri Mulpuri is a Data Analyst at 3N Consulting, where she transforms complex datasets into meaningful insights that drive smarter decisions and measurable performance improvement. With a background spanning data engineering, analytics, and cloud technologies, she brings both technical depth and strategic perspective to every engagement. Her work supports 3N's mission of helping public institutions and businesses achieve clarity, efficiency, and results through evidence-based management.

Dhatri's experience includes designing analytical models, developing dashboards, and integrating data-driven insights into organizational planning and performance assessment. Her approach blends technical precision with a strong understanding of business context—helping leaders connect metrics to mission and identify opportunities for improvement and innovation.

Expertise & Professional Background

She holds a Master of Science in Data Intelligence from the University of South Florida and has over five years of professional experience in data analytics and engineering, including prior work as a Specialist Programmer at a global IT firm.

Core Competencies: Data analytics and modeling, dashboard development, data engineering, cloud technologies, business intelligence, performance assessment, organizational planning, and evidence-based decision-making.

Dedicated to continuous learning and public value, Dhatri exemplifies 3N Consulting's commitment to excellence, transparency, and impact through data.
