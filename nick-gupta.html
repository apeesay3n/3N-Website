Meet Our Team

Nick Gupta

Chief Executive Officer

Founder of 3N Consulting LLC | 25+ Years of Management Consulting | Enterprise Transformation & Value Creation Leader

Biography

Nick Gupta is the Founder of 3N Consulting LLC, where he leads enterprise efficiency, transformation, and value-creation initiatives across the private equity, commercial, and public sectors. With more than 25 years of management consulting experience, Mr. Gupta has become a trusted advisor to CEOs, investors, and boards—helping organizations achieve measurable operational and financial improvement under aggressive growth or turnaround mandates.

Mr. Gupta's background includes extensive work with private equity portfolio companies, where he has partnered with management teams and investors to stabilize operations, accelerate EBITDA growth, and deliver post-acquisition value creation. He has served on Boards of Directors, Advisory Boards, and Technical Advisory Boards, helping firms navigate operational transformation, scale technology infrastructure, and successfully raise Series A funding. His expertise in aligning financial objectives with sustainable workforce and operational practices has made him a sought-after advisor for mid-market firms across technology, consumer goods, and manufacturing.

Career Highlights

Wipro Consulting Services: Prior to founding 3N Consulting, Mr. Gupta served as Global Head of Organizational Redesign at Wipro Consulting Services, where he was one of the fastest-tracked professionals from Manager to Partner in the firm's history. He pioneered Wipro's "Value Engineering" delivery model—offering performance assessments at no cost and sharing in verified client savings.

Early Career: His earlier career at Deloitte and BearingPoint shaped his foundation in strategy, process optimization, and financial modeling.

Major Transformations: Across his career, Mr. Gupta has led major transformation programs for Wal-Mart, BNY Mellon, NBC Universal, Citigroup, and others. At Wal-Mart's Bentonville headquarters, his redesign of the company's Information Systems Division (ISD) directly preserved 350 U.S. jobs that were slated for outsourcing—earning executive recognition for balancing efficiency with workforce protection.

Education & Background

A Maryland native, Mr. Gupta holds a B.S. in Management Science and Statistics from the University of Maryland, College Park (Scholarship Recipient) and an MBA in Finance and Strategy from Purdue University's Krannert School of Management (CIBER Assistantship Recipient).

He continues to lead 3N Consulting with a mission rooted in excellence, partnership, and measurable value creation—helping organizations realize their financial and operational potential through insight, discipline, and execution.
